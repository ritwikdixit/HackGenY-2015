Leap to Live

Play with 1 Flat, Open Hand. Make a Fist and Open to Start.

Use the leap motion to avoid the squares flying at you! To start, make a fist and then quickly flatten your hand. To use your shield, hold a fist at any time. Make sure to keep your hand flat and horizontal while playing. You start in the calibration step to center your character, then make a fist and quickly flatten your hand to start. To restart after death, simple make a circular motion with 1 finger. Try to survive as long as you can!
